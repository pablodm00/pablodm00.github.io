    Hi! I am 

    DESIGN ENGINEERING STUDENT AT IMPERIAL COLLEGE LONDON

    Ever since I was a child, I noticed design errors and things that could be improved in everyday products and services. I have always loved to look at clever features in packaging, furniture and vehicles and speculate on how they could be improved or used in different applications. I love using creativity to find new solutions to problems, and applying technical knowledge and tools to improve these solutions. That is why design engineering is the perfect field for me: a fusion of design thinking and engineering knowledge and practice.

    My love and admiration for nature has without question increased my  interest in sustainable design. I think that, as designers, we hold a special responsibility in making sure our world is in great condition for all generations to come.
